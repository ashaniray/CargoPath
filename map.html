Ocean and Sea Path Finding

Click on two points on the map so that you get two markers and then click "Draw Path" to obtain the sea path between those two points/markers.
  • Markers appear only on water or in locations close to sea
  • To delete a path or marker, click on it again.
  • You may use the box below to search for a location or you can click on the map directly to place a marker
  • Delting a marker does not "remove" the internal source/destination points
  • Currently the paths do not cross the region of 180 Longitude. Will fix that shortly
